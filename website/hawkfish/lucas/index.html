News.

21 January 2001

Lucas arrived home on Friday morning (January 19) and is already eating up a storm.  Mom and Dad are feeling a bit more like old hands this time, but sleep is still a precious thing.  Grandma Sally and Grandpa Tom have each come down to stay for a few days, and to help us all cope.  Our enernal thanks to them and to Grandma Lynn for sparing Tom in the depths of an Alaskan winter.

Lucas himself is a fairly calm baby and was born with quite an appetite.  He is feeding very well and producing enough diapers to win valuable prizes and coupons.  He is already having "quiet alert" periods when he looks around and tries to take it all in.  We expect him to be starting college in the fall!

Arthur is fairly taken with his new brother and keeps showing him toys (tools mostly) and asking to feed him and hold him.  Feeding is probably not in the picture for several weeks, but we are touched by his offers.  Arthur also is less inhibited than Mom and Dad and occasionally asks Lucas to "stop" crying!
