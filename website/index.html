The Company

Electric Fish, Inc. is a small software consultancy based in Seattle, WA. We specialize in Macintosh software development, and have experience in a wide variety of target audiences, including TCP/IP communication, multimedia, client-server and signal processing applications. We have also recently diversified into the Java and Pilot platforms.

Our main areas of expertise are:

  • Macintosh. Everything from INITs to commercial applications. We also have experience porting Windows applications to the Macintosh platform, but we do not currently do Windows development.
  • Java. Enterprise Data Mining solutions using ODBC and JavaCC. Data Visualization applets with SubArctic and Swing.
  • PalmPilot. Design and development of stragegic tools for corporate communications.

The Consultants

Company founders Richard Wesley and Dav Lion met at Brown University in 1986.

  • Richard Wesley. Mr. Wesley has ten years of commercial experience developing software for the Macintosh platform and can be reached as hawkfish@electricfish.com. Before forming Electric Fish with Mr. Lion, he was the owner of Punch Deck Consulting.
  • Dav Lion. Mr. Lion has six years commercial Macintosh development experience. Prior to forming Electric Fish, he consulted for Silicon Graphics and Microsoft.
