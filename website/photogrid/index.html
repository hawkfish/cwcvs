PhotoPress

Electric Fish is developing PhotoPress, the easiest way to print your digital photos. It will be published and distributed by Voyager. (Dealer inquiries invited at 505-474-3194).

PhotoPress will run under Mac OS 8.6 or later, and runs as a native Mac OS X application.

We're eager to have your help in testing PhotoPress before its release. We use it every day, but we want to make sure you know that it is prerelease software, and may contain bugs that could cause you to lose data. You should back up your computer before running PhotoPress.
