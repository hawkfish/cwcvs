PhotoGrid

Electric Fish is developing PhotoGrid, the easiest way to print your digital photos. It will be exclusively published and distributed by Voyager. (Dealer inquiries invited at 505-474-3194).

PhotoGrid will run under Mac OS 8.6 or later, and runs as a native Mac OS X application.
