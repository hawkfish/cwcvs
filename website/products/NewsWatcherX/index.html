NewsWatcher-X

What is it?

NewsWatcher-X is a Carbonization of John Norstad's NewsWatcher program. It runs under the Mac OS X Public Beta or with CarbonLib 1.0.4.

Where is it?

You can download it right here. The current version is 2.2.3b1.

NewsWatcher-X is also available in the following translations:

  • Français (2.2.3a5).

Does it work?

As far as I know, it mostly works, and we even have a screen shot. I developed it under OS 9.0.4 and tested it under the public beta. Not all the functionality has been tested. Several folks have stared testing it under OS X and I try to respond promptly to serious problems.

Is it documented?

The NewsWatcher 2.2.2 manual is available in both stand alone postcard format and the proprietary Microsoft Word format.

How do I report problems?

Check out the problems page,which has a list of known problems, a list of fixed problems and directions for filing bug reports.

How do I pay for it?

You don't. It is free and I did it for fun, to see how long it would take and to give something back. If you really want to pay for it, please send some money to your favorite charity. If you don't have one, try the Sanctuary Art Center.

Can I get the source?

The source for the current build is here. It requires CodeWarrior Pro 6 and Universal Headers 3.3.2 to build.
