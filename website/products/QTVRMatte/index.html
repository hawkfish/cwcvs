QTVR Matte

Description.

QTVR Matte is a plug-in for Adobe® After Effects which allows you to manipulate Quicktime VR panoramas from within the After Effects Environment, and add layers containing QTVR panoramas to a composition.

The current pre-release version of QTVRMatte also supports manipulation of QTVR Object movies, and cubic panoramas.

QTVR Matte is distributed as a plug-in which you can download immediately and try out! Licenses are are available from Kagi. You may use QTVRMatte without a license, but a green line will be drawn across each frame.

Features.

QTVR Matte includes the following features:

  • Manipulate the pan, tilt, and field-of-view of a panorama
  • Control the perspective correction of the rendered panorama
  • Control the codec quality of the rendered panorama

Requirements.

  • Adobe® After Effects 5.0 or later.
  • Mac OS based computer
  • QuickTime 3.0 or higher
  • QuickTime VR extension installed.
