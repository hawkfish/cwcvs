Color Pal Press Release

For Immediate Release

Seattle, WA -- 13 November 2001

PLUG A COLOR PALETTE INTO AFTER EFFECTS

Electric Fish, Inc. is pleased to announce the release of Color Pal for Adobe After Effects. Color Pal is a floating palette that provides quick access to a user-configurable set of colors from within After Effects.

Color Pal can be used with any standard eye-dropper in After Effects (e.g. composition background color, all color effect controls, solid color). Just click on the eyedropper then click on the color of your choice in Color Pal.

"Video artists told us that Color Pal is a utility that should have been part of After Effects," said Dav Lion, Co-President of Electric Fish. "Now they can have it as a seamless part of the application."

Color Pal works with After Effects 5.0 or later, and is available for both Macintosh and Windows.

A fully functional 7-day trial version of Color Pal is available from Electric Fish's web site at <http://www.electricfish.com/products/colorpal/>. Users can purchase and register Color Pal directly from the plug-in for $20.00.
