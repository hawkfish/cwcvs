PhotoPressTM Press Release

For Immediate Release

Seattle, WA -- 12 November 2001

NOW IT'S EASY TO PRINT DIGITAL PHOTOS

Electric Fish, Inc. is pleased to announce the release of Electric Fish PhotoPress, the easiest way to print digital photos. PhotoPress runs under Mac OS 8.6 or later, and is native for OS X. PhotoPress features drag and drop importing of your digital photos, with fast and simple choices of layouts to help optimize your use of photo paper, and various ways of labelling your images.

"PhotoPress finally fulfills the promise of digital photography," said Dav Lion, Co-President of Electric Fish. "By making it dead simple to output your snapshots to any color printer, it solidifies the Macintosh's position as a digital hub."

PhotoPress features a unique and powerful cropping tool. And it includes helpful management functions like renaming cryptic files, creating previews, and making Finder icons. "PhotoPress is the perfect tool for anyone with a digital camera."

A 30-day free trial version of PhotoPress is available from Electric Fish's web site at <http://www.electricfish.com/products/photopress/>. Users can buy a licensed copy for $24.95 simply by running the application.
