Electric Fish, Inc.

Services

Electric Fish, Inc. specializes in Macintosh software development, and has experience in a wide variety of areas, including TCP/IP communication, games, multimedia, data visualization, client-server and signal processing applications. We've shipped a number of shrink-wrapped applications for the consumer market. We also have experience with the Unix, Java and Pilot platforms.

Our main areas of expertise are:

  • Macintosh. Everything from INITs to commercial applications, including Carbon and OS X. We also have experience porting Windows applications to the Macintosh platform, but we do not currently do Windows development.
  • Java. We are experienced in a variety of Java technologies including Swing, CORBA, and ODBC. We've delivered both applets and standalone applications for Linux, Mac OS, and Windows.
  • Palm Pilot. Design and development of strategic tools for corporate communications.
  • Macintosh Version Control. Products, support and information on most Macintosh version control systems.
