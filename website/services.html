Electric Fish, Inc.

Services

Electric Fish, Inc. is specializes in Macintosh software development, and have experience in a wide variety of target audiences, including TCP/IP communication, multimedia, client-server and signal processing applications. We also have experience with the Unix, Java and Pilot platforms.

Our main areas of expertise are:

  • Macintosh. Everything from INITs to commercial applications. We also have experience porting Windows applications to the Macintosh platform, but we do not currently do Windows development.
  • Java. Enterprise data mining solutions using ODBC and JavaCC. Data Visualization applets with SubArctic and Swing.
  • PalmPilot. Design and development of stragegic tools for corporate communications.
  • Macintosh Version Control. Products, support and information on most Macintosh version control systems.
