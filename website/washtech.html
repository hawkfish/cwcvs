Electric Fish Friends

Electric Fish supports the efforts of the Washington Alliance of Technology Workers to empower technical workers. We are a Union shop and support the efforts of workers everywhere to regain control over their lives.

The following businesses are staffed by WashTech members:

  • The Bartley Consulting Group. Office Automation and Microsoft Access Applications.
  • DCIPRO Corporation. Technical consulting for Microsoft Windows and Intel hardware.
  • Small Systems Company. Multiple role engineering development support for technology startups.
  • Usable Webs. Web site design, site usability, documentation.

Electric Fish also supports the efforts of worker controlled businesses in other states:

  • The Professional Association of Contract Employees. California based umbrella company for contractors.
